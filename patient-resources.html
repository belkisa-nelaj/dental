Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?

For most patients, we recommend scheduling a dental check-up and professional cleaning every six months. However, depending on your specific oral health needs, we may suggest more frequent visits.

What should I do in case of a dental emergency?

If you're experiencing a dental emergency, call our office immediately at (555) 123-4567. For after-hours emergencies, follow the prompts to reach our on-call dentist. For severe emergencies involving facial trauma or difficulty breathing, please go to the nearest emergency room.

How can I manage dental anxiety?

We understand that dental anxiety is common. Our office offers several comfort options including noise-cancelling headphones, blankets, and various sedation options for patients who experience anxiety. Let us know about your concerns before your appointment, and we'll work with you to make your visit as comfortable as possible.

Do you offer payment plans for major procedures?

Yes, we offer several financing options including in-house payment plans and third-party financing through CareCredit. Our financial coordinator will work with you to find the best option for your budget.

What should I do if I chip or break a tooth?

If you chip or break a tooth, rinse your mouth with warm water and apply a cold compress to reduce swelling. Save any broken pieces if possible. Call our office as soon as possible to schedule an emergency appointment. We'll assess the damage and recommend appropriate treatment options.
