Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our general dentistry services

How often should I visit the dentist for a checkup?

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for regular checkups and cleanings. However, depending on your oral health needs, our dentists may suggest more frequent visits.

Do you see children? At what age should kids start seeing a dentist?

Yes, we welcome patients of all ages! Children should have their first dental visit by their first birthday or when their first tooth appears, whichever comes first. Early visits help establish good oral hygiene habits and make dental appointments a positive experience.

Are digital X-rays safe?

Yes, digital X-rays are very safe. They use up to 90% less radiation than traditional film X-rays. We also use protective equipment to minimize exposure. The diagnostic benefits of X-rays far outweigh the minimal risks.

What payment options do you offer?

We accept most major dental insurance plans and offer various payment options including credit cards, cash, and flexible payment plans. Our front desk team will help you understand your coverage and estimate any out-of-pocket costs before treatment.

What should I do in case of a dental emergency?

Contact our office immediately at (555) 123-4567. We reserve time in our daily schedule for emergency cases and will do our best to see you the same day. Outside of office hours, our answering service will direct you to an on-call dentist.
